Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to display the cattle available for purchase from Rees Livestock. The site will showcase the best animals available on the front page and then there will be a second page where users will be able to view and sort through all the animals available from the ranch.

Audience

The target audience will be for 4H people looking to buy a club animal and show it. The ranch is based in Star Valley so this site is primarily for people in the surounding area but can also be helpful for others as well.

Dynamic elements

The livestock and all the data for them will be stored in a javascript object and will be dynamically displayed on the screen on both pages. The Homepage will show the featured animals and the "animals" page will show all the animals. Filter options will be available for users to filter through the animals
