Our Small Family Ranch

Rees Livestock is a small, family-owned and operated ranch located in the beautiful Star Valley, Wyoming. For the past 15 years, we've been dedicated to raising high-quality club calves, offering top-notch livestock to both seasoned ranchers and newcomers alike. Our commitment to excellence and personalized care ensures that every calf raised on our ranch meets the highest standards, contributing to the success of our customers' operations.
